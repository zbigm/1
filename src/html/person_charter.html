CONSTITUTION OF AAECA
Chapter 1 General Rules
Article 1. The name of the Association is American Asian Econo- mic and Cultural Association or AAECA.
Article 2. The Association shall be an independent, non-political and non-profit organization which is voluntarily formed by BOHONG INC.
Article 3. The Association was founded in February, 2014, registered in San Francisco, U.S.A.
Article 4. The purpose for which the corporation is formed are: (1) To promote American Asian economic and cultural development; (2) To establish win-win platform.
